Let's Play PIG!

Rules

  • Players take turns rolling the die.
  • If the player rolls a 1, they lose all points earned during their turn.
  • If the player holds, points earned during their turn are added to their total score.
  • The player's turn ends when the player rolls a 1 or chooses to hold.
  • First player to a total score of 100 wins.
